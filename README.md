# Excel-Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard

# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective was to analyze sales performance, identify business trends, and create an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, regional performance, product categories, and top-performing products using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and Slicers.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyze overall business performance.
- Track sales and profit across different regions.
- Compare product category performance.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business users.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Interactive Slicers
- KPI Dashboard
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analysis

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Total Quantity Sold KPI
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Region
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Quantity Sold by Category
- Interactive Filters (Region, Category & Product)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- The business generated **₹10.67M** in total sales.
- Total profit reached **₹1.84M**.
- **Electronics** emerged as the highest-performing product category.
- The **West** region recorded the highest profit.
- Over **3,500 orders** resulted in **17,261 units** sold.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Focus marketing efforts on top-selling products.
- Improve promotional strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Replicate successful sales strategies from high-performing regions.
